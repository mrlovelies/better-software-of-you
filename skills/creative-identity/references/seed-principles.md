# Seed Narrative Principles

From the first creative session (Braska's Pilgrimage, March 2026).

## Structure

1. **Indirect revelation over direct introspection** (weight: 0.9)
   Let the reader piece together emotional truth from observed behavior rather than stated feeling.
   *Evidence:* The scalp tingle came from Braska observing Auron's architecture and seeing how it would be dismantled through a smile.

2. **Dramatic irony as primary emotional engine** (weight: 0.9)
   Reader knowledge doing work the prose doesn't state. The reader knowing where the pilgrimage ends makes every warm moment devastating.

3. **Closing paragraphs that land through nostalgic anticipation + empathy simultaneously** (weight: 0.85)
   Pattern identified across multiple draft endings.

## Pacing

4. **Earned interiority** (weight: 0.8)
   Characters shouldn't be overly introspective before the narrative earns it. Restraint early, depth later. Early chapters build through external observation; interior access is a reward the prose earns.

## Character

5. **Character opacity as a tool** (weight: 0.85)
   Some characters are more powerful when never given POV — described only through the disruption they cause in others. Jecht never gets POV; always described through reactions he provokes in Auron and Braska.

6. **"Correct" as containment system** (weight: 0.8)
   Character voice using specific repeated words as psychological architecture. Auron's use of "correct" as emotional armor.

## POV

7. **Observer POV can be more emotionally devastating than internal POV** (weight: 0.85)
   Braska watching Auron can hit harder than being inside Auron's head. Discovery from comparing three POV versions of the same scene.

## Tone

8. **Sentimental yet aggressive** (weight: 0.8)
   These registers don't need to resolve into one tone. Let them coexist. Tenderness and violence existing in the same narrative space without flattening either.
